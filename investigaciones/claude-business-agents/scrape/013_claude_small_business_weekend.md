# How to Use Claude to Automate Your Small Business in a Weekend

Source: https://medium.com/@sebuzdugan/how-to-use-claude-to-automate-your-small-business-in-a-weekend-15c749aac5e0

Most small businesses do not need "AI strategy." They need fewer tabs, fewer repetitive tasks, and fewer "Can you quickly draft this?" pings on Slack.

Claude for Small Business is basically Anthropic saying: you do not need an ML team to get real value from a frontier model. You can treat Claude like a very fast, very patient co-worker that lives in your browser, your docs, and your workflows.

If you are technical, the interesting part is this. You can wire Claude into your business in layers, starting from zero code and moving toward API automations when it actually makes sense.

## What "Claude for Small Business" Actually Is

There is no separate "Claude for Small Business" SKU. It is a positioning of the existing Claude offerings for small and medium businesses.

In practice, you are looking at three main surfaces: the web app, the Team / Pro plans, and the API or integrations.

The models behind all of this are the Claude 3 family: Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku. They share useful traits for small teams: strong language ability, long context.
