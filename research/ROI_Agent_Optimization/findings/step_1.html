Hallazgos principales

Las 7 formas en que AI automation genera profit

  • Lead generation — More leads = more sales (30-45 días ROI)
  • Customer service bots — Fewer support staff needed (14-30 días ROI)
  • Content creation — Marketing sin contratar writers (30-60 días ROI)
  • Sales follow-ups — No leads fall through cracks (21-30 días ROI)
  • Data entry automation — Staff time freed for revenue work (7-14 días ROI)
  • Email marketing — Higher open rates, more conversions (30-45 días ROI)
  • Appointment scheduling — No back-and-forth, fewer no-shows (7-14 días ROI)

El math básicos

"Si gastas 10 horas/semana en tasks que AI puede handle, y tu tiempo vale $100/hora, eso es $4,000/mes que estás ahorrando o freed up para focus en growth."

Best practices para maximize ROI

1. Start with high-impact workflows
  • Focus en processes que son: high-volume, repetitive, y mission-critical
  • No automatizar todo — solo lo que mueve el needle
  • Empezar con 1 automation que solve un clear problem
2. Diseñar para orchestration, no solo automation
  • One agent answering questions = helpful
  • Network de agents coordinating workflow = transformative
  • Agentic workflows permiten autonomous AI agents ejecutar full processes across tools
3. Conectar a sistemas existentes, no rebuild
  • No rip out what's already working
  • Integrar, no rebuild
  • ROI se derrumba si intentas rebuild todo desde cero
4. Medir con KPIs duros
  • Hard ROI KPIs = concrete financial data: costs saved or profits gained
  • Labor cost reductions: hours saved due to automation
  • Revenue enhancement: new product introductions
  • Customer experience improvement

Framework para decidir qué automatizar

Strong Automation Candidate:
  • Happens daily or weekly across multiple people ✅
  • Follows recognizable pattern with defined inputs ✅
  • Affects revenue, cycle time, margin, risk, or customer experience ✅
  • Source documents, CRM records, tickets are accessible ✅
  • Human can approve, reject, or correct output ✅
Weak Automation Candidate:
  • Happens rarely or only for edge cases ❌
  • Requires unusual judgment every time ❌
  • Saves a few minutes with no measurable downstream effect ❌
  • Knowledge lives mostly in people's heads ❌
  • No one owns quality control after launch ❌
