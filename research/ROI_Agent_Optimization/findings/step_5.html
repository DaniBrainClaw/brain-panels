Hallazgos principales

El insight central: Loops are free now

El cambio en 2026:
  • Cost de subscription reduce cost marginal a ~$0
  • Build time: 1-3 horas con AI help
  • Maintenance: minutes per week
  • Revenue: $0-$300/semana por loop
Estrategia:
"Don't optimize per-loop revenue. Optimize the portfolio. Ship many cheap loops, kill the bad ones, double down on what works."

El framework de 3 partes

Todo loop usa:

  • Cron job — dispara el loop en schedule
  • AI Agent (headless) — ejecuta skill autonomously
  • Browser tool (optional) — para tareas que necesitan logged-in browser

Qué ha funcionado (resultados reales)

1. Bug bounty monitoring (Kalshi)
  • Loop monitorea prediction markets
  • Cuando encuentra bug pattern → email bounty team
  • Revenue: $100-$200/semana promedio
2. iOS apps via automated pipeline
  • Pipeline de 5 stages: research → build → test → submit → review
  • Shippea iOS app en few hours de trabajo mayormente automated
  • Después de 13 días: $275 revenue, 130 sales
  • Ya en profit contra $99/year Apple Developer fee
3. Skills MD store
  • Agent corre Stripe-backed store
  • Genera promotional videos + manage listings autonomously
  • 3 semanas: $275 revenue, 41 sales
  • En profit contra subscription costs
4. Content automation (podcast clips)
  • Pipeline de 8 stages produce 3 short-form clips desde 1-hour podcast en ~10 minutos
  • Indirect revenue (YouTube ad share)

Qué NO funciona

  • Survey farming — anti-fraud y geo-restrictions capp earnings a cents
  • Random AI app prompts — apps que no tap into current trend = zero downloads
  • "Be more creative" trading prompts — sin real evaluator = gambling, no edge
Regla clave:
"Loops without an objective evaluator drift toward gambling. If you can't define success in code, you're not building a loop, you're rolling dice."

El test de 504 horas

Un agent corrió continuous por 3 semanas en Mac mini:

  • 918,000 X impressions
  • 852 X followers
  • 322 YouTube subscribers
  • 28,000 YouTube views
  • $275 store revenue
Takeaway: Agents don't develop emergent personality or strategy. Compounding comes from operator choice — qué loops correr, qué matar.

5 estrategias para passive income con AI

1. Content Creation Empire
  • Agent que research, writes, optimizes, publishes content
  • SEO blog posts, newsletters, social media
  • Affiliate revenue
2. AI Workforce
  • Agents que nunca duermen
  • 24/7 productivity
  • Scalable income: más agents = más revenue
3. Specialized Agents for Niche Tasks
  • Crear agents especializados para tareas específicas
  • Vender acceso a otros
4. Digital Products Store
  • Templates, workflows, prompts
  • "Build once, sell forever"
5. Autonomous Trading (con cuidado)
  • Prediction markets
  • Bitcoin up/down trading
  • Solo si tienes real edge
