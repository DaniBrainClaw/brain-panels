Modelos de Negocio Viables para BRAIN

1. AI Automation Service (Agency Model)

Descripción: Vender servicios de automatización AI a businesses. Pricing:
  • Discovery session: $150-$500
  • Implementation project: $1,000-$5,000
  • Monthly retainer: $500-$3,000
  • High-ticket: $5,000-$20,000
Casos documentados:
  • $47K en 90 días (14 clientes × $800-$3,500/mes)
  • £80K/mes (Dan Gibbs, UK)
  • $130K/mes (Andy Walters, South Africa)
Best niches:
  • Real estate agents
  • Dental/medical offices
  • Law firms
  • E-commerce
  • Fitness coaches

2. Research as a Service

Descripción: deliverables de investigación personalizados. Pricing:
  • Research reports: $500-$2,000/proyecto
  • Battlecards: $200-$500/set
  • Monthly monitoring: $500-$1,500/mes
  • Custom CI project: $2,000-$10,000
Diferenciador: Speed (M2.7 research rápido) + Price (más barato que consulting firms).

3. Coding Agent Service

Descripción: AI agent para development tasks autonomously. Pricing:
  • Subscription: $500-$3,000/mes
  • Usage-based: $0.10-$0.50/ejecución
  • Project-based: $2,000-$10,000/proyecto
Stack: BRAIN + M2.7 para coding + Codex para execution.

4. Subscription Bundle (Todo-en-uno)

Descripción: Todo ilimitado (agents, usage, support). Pricing: $3,000-$5,000/mes Casos documentados: El modelo más exitoso para solopreneurs vendiendo AI agents.
