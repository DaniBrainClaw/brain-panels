## Hallazgos principales

### Framework para vender AI automation services

**Regla #1: Niche down**
"Pick a niche. If you're selling 'AI automation,' that's too broad. Businesses pay for solutions, not vague tech."

**Framework de venta (DM Strategy):**
- Message 20-30 niche businesses per day
- Pitches tied to one specific pain point
- Ejemplo: "Want to automate client onboarding?"
- Alt: "What tasks take up most of your team's time?"

### Best niches para AI automation (2026)

1. **Real estate agents** — lead follow-up, appointment scheduling, document management
2. **Dental/medical offices** — appointment reminders, rebooking, patient intake
3. **Law firms** — client intake, document requests, follow-up emails
4. **E-commerce brands** — abandoned cart, order status, customer service FAQs
5. **Fitness coaches/gyms** — onboarding, scheduling, check-in messages
6. **Recruitment agencies** — candidate follow-ups, application status, interview scheduling

### Cómo estructurar el offer

**Step 1: Discovery session ($150-$500)**
- Audit de workflow del cliente
- Identificar 3-5 procesos repetitivos que cuestan tiempo
- Presentar plan para automatizarlos

**Step 2: Implementation project ($1,000-$5,000)**
- Construir la automation
- Testear y запускать
- Training al cliente

**Step 3: Monthly retainer ($500-$3,000/mes)**
- Mantenimiento y mejoras
- Garantizar que funciona
- Upsell de nuevas automations

### El pitch que funciona

**Say this:**
"I help businesses automate repetitive work with AI. What tasks take up most of your team's time?"

**Lo que NO decir:**
- "I sell AI" (demasiado vago)
- "I can automate everything" (overpromising)
- Tecnología sin contexto de negocio

## Resumen

El secreto para vender AI automation services:
1. **Niche down** — un vertical específico
2. **One pain point** — una problema concreto a resolver
3. **Demo con ROI** — mostrar time saved o money earned
4. **Start small** — $1K-$3K para primero proyecto, luego retainer

Dani puede empezar concold outreach a 20-30 negocios por día en su nicho target.
