| Line | Amount | Requirements | Best For |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|
| **Emprendedores** | €25K-300K | Company <24 months old; own funds ≥ loan amount | Startups |

| **Startup y Pymes** | €25K-1.5M | Innovative project, balanced financial structure | Growth phase |
| **Emprendedoras Digitales** | €25K-1.5M | Digital projects led by women | Female founders |

### Key Terms
- **Interest:** Euribor + 2-4.25% (first tranche) + 0-8% based on profitability (second tranche)
- **Maturity:** Up to 7-9 years
- **Grace period:** Up to 5-7 years
- **Opening commission:** 0.5%
- **No personal guarantee required** (unlike banks)

### Eligibility for TERRACOTTA
- Must be registered as SME (S.L. or similar)
- Innovative project (social wellness club qualifies as novel in Spain)
- Own funds must equal loan amount (for most lines)
- Real estate excluded, but wellness services are eligible

### Alternative Financing
- **ICF (Institut Català de Finances):** For Catalonia-based companies
- **Business angels Spain:** Wellness/health sector investors
- **Crowdfunding:** platforms like Verkami, Crowdcube

## 4. Commercial Lease Early Termination (Spain)

### Legal Framework
- **Contract-driven:** LAU (Urban Leases Law) governs; contract terms take precedence
- **Penalty flexibility:** Parties can negotiate freely
- **Typical penalties:** 1 month's rent per remaining year of contract (or proportional)

### Negotiation Recommendations
1. **Aim for:** 3-month notice period + penalty proportional to remaining contract
2. **Avoid:** Long fixed-term leases (5+ years) without break clauses
3. **Preferred:** 3+3+3 (3-year initial, then annual renewals)

## 5. Energy Costs (Madrid 2026)

### Commercial Tariff 3.0TD (over 15kW)
- **SME range:** €0.14-0.23/kWh
- **Time-of-use pricing:** P1 (peak) most expensive, P6 (off-peak) cheapest
- **Off-peak rates:** Can drop to €0.04-0.07/kWh

### For Sauna + Cold Plunge
- **Estimated monthly (200sqm):** €800-1,500 depending on usage patterns
- **Heat recovery:** Can save up to 25% on heating costs
- **Cold plunge chiller:** ~$25-30/month (2HP+ commercial)

### Energy Optimization Strategies
1. Run sauna during off-peak hours (P5-P6)
2. Install heat recovery from sauna exhaust to warm incoming air
3. Use insulated covers on cold plunge when not in use
4. Zone heating for different areas
