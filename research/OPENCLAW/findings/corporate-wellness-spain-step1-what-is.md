## 1. What Is Corporate Wellness?

**Definition:** Corporate wellness (bienestar corporativo) = employer-funded programs designed to support healthy behavior in the workplace.

In Spain, it's a strategic approach that goes beyond offering basic benefits. It involves implementing policies, actions, and tools aimed at improving employees' overall quality of life and creating a work environment that supports their physical, mental, and emotional health.

### Key Characteristics

- **Holistic approach:** Physical, mental, emotional, and social well-being
- **Strategic importance:** Companies view it as fundamental for talent attraction/retention
- **Employee demand:** Significant portion consider wellness services essential when choosing an employer
- **ROI evidence:** Every €1 invested can generate up to €11 in return; can decrease absenteeism by up to 39%

## 7. Step 1 Conclusions

**Corporate wellness in Spain = €1.2-1.7B market** with 8-12% annual growth, dominated by health insurers (Adeslas, Sanitas, Mapfre) and EAP providers.

**TERRACOTTA can capture this market** through:
- Corporate memberships (€59-69/employee/month)
- Team wellness days (€80-200/person)
- Annual partnerships (€5,000-15,000/year)

**Key insight:** The "social wellness experience" (sauna + cold plunge + community) is NOT currently offered by major corporate wellness providers in Spain — this is a **first-mover opportunity**.
