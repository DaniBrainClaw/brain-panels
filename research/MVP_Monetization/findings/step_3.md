## Hallazgos principales

### Los números que importan
- Avg. landing page CVR: 8-15%
- Headline impact on CVR: 40%+
- 20 CTA variations to test
- Build time with templates: <4 hours

### El framework de 9 bloques

**Block 1: Hero Section**
- Headline: [Outcome] for [Target Client] — Without [Pain Point]
- Subhead: [Service] that [primary benefit] in [timeframe]
- Primary CTA: [Action Verb] + [Outcome]
- Ejemplo: "AI Workflows That Save 20+ Hours Per Week — Built in 14 Days"

**Block 2: Pain Agitation**
- "Sound Familiar?"
- 3-4 pain points específicos
- Transición: "There's a better way."

**Block 3: Solution Introduction**
- Bridge from problem to solution
- Positioning statement claro

**Block 4: Benefits Stack**
- 6 beneficios enfocados en outcomes
- No features — resultados específicos

**Block 5: Social Proof**
- Testimonials, case studies, client logos, metrics
- Elimina risk perception

**Block 6: Pricing**
- Tiered pricing con deliverables claros
- Frame value, no solo precio

**Block 7: FAQ**
- 5-7 objection-handling questions
- Preemptive answers

**Block 8: Final CTA**
- Restated headline + urgency + primary + secondary CTAs

**Block 9: Trust Footer**
- Guarantee, contact info, credentials

### Headline formulas que funcionan

**Fórmula 1:**
"[Outcome] for [Target Client] — Without [Pain Point]"
→ "AI Workflows That Save 20+ Hours Per Week — Without Hiring an Assistant"

**Fórmula 2:**
"[Service description] that [primary benefit] in [timeframe]"
→ "Custom automation systems that eliminate manual data entry in 14 days"

**Fórmula 3:**
"Trusted by [number] [client type] since [year]"
→ "Trusted by 40+ agencies since 2024"

### Pain Agitation template

```
Section Headline: "Sound Familiar?"

Pain Point 1: "You spend [X hours/week] manually [task] when AI could handle it in [fraction of time]."

Pain Point 2: "Your team wastes [resource] on [repetitive process] instead of [high-value activity]."

Pain Point 3: "You've tried [common failed solution] but it [specific shortcoming]."

Pain Point 4: "Every week you delay costs you [quantified loss]."
```

### CTA templates

**Primary CTAs:**
- "Book Your Free Automation Audit"
- "Get Your AI Workflow Assessment"
- "Start Your Free Trial"

**Secondary CTAs:**
- "See How It Works"
- "Watch a 2-Minute Demo"
- "View Case Study"

## Resumen para Dani — ACTION PLAN

**Build tu landing page en <4 horas:**

1. **Hero (30 min)**
   - Headline: "[Resultado] para [cliente] — Sin [dolor]"
   - Subhead: "[Servicio] que [beneficio] en [tiempo]"
   - CTA: "Book Tu Free Audit"

2. **Pain Agitation (30 min)**
   - 4 pain points específicos de tu niche
   - Usa números reales ("12 horas/semana")

3. **Solution + Benefits (30 min)**
   - Tu servicio como respuesta
   - 6 beneficios con resultados específicos

4. **Social Proof (20 min)**
   - Aunque sea 1 testimonial
   - Metrics si tienes

5. **Pricing (20 min)**
   - 3 tiers: €500/€1,500/€3,000
   - Deliverables claros por tier

6. **FAQ + CTA (20 min)**
   - 5 objections comunes
   - CTA final con urgency
